## Status Definitions

| Status      | Meaning                                                              |
|-------------|----------------------------------------------------------------------|
| `Active`    | Device is fully registered, documented, and operational in the Haven |
| `Pending`   | Device registration is in progress                                   |
| `Dormant`   | Device is registered but not currently active                        |
| `Archived`  | Device has been decommissioned; Domo is preserved for history        |

---

## Device Types

| Type       | Meaning                                                              |
|------------|----------------------------------------------------------------------|
| `Origin`   | The first device. The anchor of the Haven's timeline.                |
| `Primary`  | A core, always-on device in the Haven                                |
| `Secondary`| A supporting or specialized device                                   |
| `Agent`    | A software-defined or AI-driven device                               |
| `Virtual`  | A logical device without dedicated hardware                          |
