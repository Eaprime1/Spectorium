## Status Definitions

| Status      | Meaning                                                                   |
|-------------|---------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| `Active`    | Grove is fully registered, documented, and operational in the Spectorium  |
| `Pending`   | Grove registration is in progress                                         |
| `Dormant`   | Grove is registered but device is not currently active                    |
| `Archived`  | Device decommissioned; Grove preserved for the historical record          |
