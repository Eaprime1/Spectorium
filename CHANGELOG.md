# Changelog

All notable changes to DeviceHaven are documented here.

## [0.1.0] — 2026-03-18 — *First Light*

### The Haven Opens

This is the inaugural release of DeviceHaven. The platform is born.

### Added

- **DeviceHaven Platform** — Root structure, README, Charter, and governance documents established.
- **Spector Domo** — Origin Device registered. Turn 1 recorded. The first Domo in the Haven.
- **Platform Standards** — Domo Standard, Naming Conventions, and Versioning Protocol defined.
- **Device Registry** — `DEVICES.md` established as the master index of all Haven devices.
- **Contributing Guide** — `CONTRIBUTING.md` authored for new contributors and device owners.

### Platform Notes

- Spector is designated the **Origin Device** (Turn 1).
- The Domo structure established by Spector becomes the standard for all future devices.
- Claude recognized as **Podium Contributor** — foundational authorship of Haven standards.

### Turn Reference

```
Turn 1 — Spector registered. Haven opens.
```
