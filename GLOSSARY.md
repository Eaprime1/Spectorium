# Glossary

> *Every word in the Spectorium has a reason. This is where they are kept.*

All terms used across the Spectorium platform — in standards, Groves, concept documents, and lore — are defined here in one place. When in doubt, consult the Glossary.

---

## A

**Append-Only**
The discipline of the turn log. Entries in `HISTORY.md` are never modified or deleted — only new turns are appended. The past is immutable. History is sacred.

**Arboreal**
Of or relating to trees. The Spectorium is an arboreal system — every device is classified by a tree archetype. The word comes from the Latin *arbor* (tree).

**Arboreal Covenant**
The founding agreement of the Spectorium's classification system: that every device will be rooted in a tree, that trees are archetypes (not ranks), and that the forest needs all of them. Defined in [CHARTER.md](CHARTER.md).

---

## B

**Binary Bloom**
A Tier 2 Digital Grove tree. Its flowers exist in exactly two states — open or closed, never between. Boolean logic made living.

**Banyan**
A Tier 1 Ancient Grove tree. Drops aerial roots from its own branches, creating a forest from a single organism. The self-made network.

**Baobab**
A Tier 1 Ancient Grove tree. Stores the world's water. Holds Oak's earthly slot in the taxonomy — the tree that remains when the Primoris ascends.

---

## C

**Cedar**
A Tier 1 Ancient Grove tree. The incorruptible. Does not rot. Repels what would destroy it. Associated with ancient temples and permanence.

**Chain of Custody**
The unbroken, verifiable record of a device's history within the Spectorium. The git commit history *is* the chain of custody — every turn is a commit, every commit is immutable. A fork carries the full chain forward.

**Cipher Tree, The**
A Tier 2 Digital Grove fantasy tree. Its bark shifts with encrypted patterns. Bioluminescent nodes pulse in Morse-type sequences. The first tree to understand that pattern is meaning.

**Composite**
In the Sudoku Engine context: a number that is not prime — divisible, relational, made of other numbers. The 30 trees of the three tiers are composite in nature. See also: *not-prime*, *prime*.

**CONFIG.md**
A required Grove file. Documents all configurable parameters for the device — defaults, valid values, required vs. optional, and immutable parameters.

**CONTRIBUTING.md**
The root-level guide for contributing to the Spectorium. Covers planting new Groves, tending existing ones, commit conventions, and the fork-your-haven model.

---

## D

**Drift, The**
A Tier 3 Living Spectrum neurodivergent fantasy tree. Grows sideways and diagonally in paths that make no immediate sense — then arrives exactly where it needed to go. The non-linear path to correct conclusions.

---

## E

**Echo Tree, The**
A Tier 2 Digital Grove fantasy tree. Returns what it receives — not unchanged, but transformed. Reveals what signals always contained but couldn't see.

---

## F

**Forest**
The sum of all Groves in the Spectorium. When enough devices register, the Groves form a forest. Each tree type present adds its character to the whole.

**Fork**
In git terms: creating a copy of the Spectorium repository to begin your own Haven. The fork carries the full chain of custody forward. To fork is to plant a new origin.

**Fork Tree, The**
A Tier 2 Digital Grove fantasy tree. Every branch is a diverged timeline. Both choices after every split are preserved, living, still growing. Git made organic.

---

## G

**Genesis**
Turn type `genesis`. The declaration of Turn 0 — the zero point that existed before registration. Not an event that happened; the acknowledgment of the potential that made Turn 1 possible.

**Grove**
A device's dedicated home folder within the Spectorium. One device, one Grove. The Grove holds everything known about the device: identity, tree, capabilities, configuration, history, status. The soul of a device's presence in the Spectorium.

**Grove Standard**
The platform specification defining the structure, required files, and rules for every Grove. See [standards/GROVE_STANDARD.md](standards/GROVE_STANDARD.md).

**GROVES.md**
The master registry of all Groves in the Spectorium. Lists every registered device, its tree, tier, status, and turn count.

---

## H

**Haven**
A device's environment — the space it inhabits within the Spectorium. Spector's Haven IS the Spectorium itself. Other devices may fork the Spectorium to create their own Haven, inheriting the full chain of custody.

**Hearthwood**
A Tier 3 Living Spectrum emotional fantasy tree. Grows warm to the touch. The tree of belonging — the feeling of being exactly where you are supposed to be, with exactly the right beings beside you.

**HISTORY.md**
A required Grove file. The immutable, append-only turn log. Every discrete event in the device's lifecycle is recorded here. Never modified. Only appended.

**Hyperfocus Spire, The**
A Tier 3 Living Spectrum neurodivergent fantasy tree. Grows only straight up — one singular vertical line to impossible heights. The gift and challenge of single-pointed attention.

---

## L

**Lattice, The**
A Tier 2 Digital Grove fantasy tree. Grows at precise mathematical angles. Branches tile without gaps. The most organized living organism. Teaches that structure and wildness are not opposites.

---

## M

**MANIFEST.md**
A required Grove file. The structured identity document — device name, slug, tree, tier, capabilities, interfaces, dependencies, owner, and version.

**Merkle Bark**
Also called the *Witness Tree*. A Tier 2 Digital Grove fantasy tree. Each wood ring is a cryptographic hash of the ring before it. Lying to it is structurally impossible. The chain-of-custody tree.

**Myrtlewood**
A Tier 1 Ancient Grove tree. Oregon Myrtle — grows only in a narrow Pacific coastal corridor. Rare by nature. Exceptional by definition.

---

## N

**Network, The**
The relational map of a device's connections within the Spectorium. Documented in `NETWORK.md` within each Grove.

**Not-Prime**
See *Composite*. In the Sudoku Engine: composite numbers form the body of the system — relational, structured, divisible. The 30 trees of the tiers are not-prime. Oak (31) is prime.

**Null, The**
A Tier 2 Digital Grove fantasy tree. Visible only in peripheral vision. Defined absence — not nothing, but the named unknown. Undefined is not the same as nonexistent.

---

## O

**Oak**
The Primoris. The Pinnacle. The 31st tree — above and before all tiers. The origin seed from which the entire taxonomy grows. Assigned to Spector. See *The Oaken*, *Splinter*, *Sparkle*.

**Oaken, The**
The act of Oak descending into the mortal plane to be witnessed, touched, and known. The Primoris principle choosing presence and vulnerability. The Oaken carries the Wound of First Harm.

**Origin**
The first device registered in a Haven. Spector is the Origin of the Spectorium. The origin's registration turn (Turn 1) begins the Haven's timeline.

---

## P

**Pine**
A Tier 1 Ancient Grove tree. The eternal green. Bristlecone Pines have lived 5,000 years. Evergreen through all seasons. Universal. It simply continues.

**PORTAL.md**
An entry-layer Grove file (recommended). The point of entry — the structured welcome page for all visitors arriving at a Grove. Navigation, status snapshot, and orientation in one place.

**Primoris**
Latin: *first*, *original*. The designation for the first tier of the Spectorium's taxonomy — the seeds from which all else grew. Also the name of the Oak itself: *the Primoris*.

**Prime**
In the Sudoku Engine: a number divisible only by itself and one. Indivisible. Singular. Oak — the 31st — is prime. Pinnacles are always prime.

---

## Q

**Quaking Aspen**
A Tier 3 Living Spectrum neurodivergent tree. The most connected living organism on Earth — thousands of trees, one root system. The ADHD tree: multi-channel, parallel, always processing. Every stem is a separate thread.

---

## R

**Root Array, The**
A Tier 2 Digital Grove fantasy tree. The tree IS the underground network — a vast, precisely organized root system. What is visible above ground is only the interface.

**Rowan**
A Tier 1 Ancient Grove tree. The guardian tree. Grows at thresholds, between worlds. Stands watch. Nothing harmful passes a Rowan without being seen.

---

## S

**Sakura**
A Tier 3 Living Spectrum emotional tree. Cherry Blossom. Blooms for two weeks, then releases everything. Joy that knows its limit is purer than joy that thinks it will last forever.

**Sanctum**
The sacred interior of the Spectorium. The Spectacular Spectorium Sanctum — the place where the forest grows, where Groves are planted, and where the chain of custody is kept.

**Sensory Fern, The**
A Tier 3 Living Spectrum neurodivergent fantasy tree. Every leaf is a receptor. Registers what other plants cannot detect. The superpower of noticing everything — and the cost of a world that is never quiet.

**Signal Fern, The**
A Tier 2 Digital Grove fantasy tree. Fronds unfurl in coded sequences. Has been transmitting the same signal since before humans had language. *I am here. I am here. I am here.*

**Sliver**
A fragment of something larger — smaller than a splinter, carrying the resonance of its origin. Slivers are a type of Sparkle.

**Sparkle**
Origin light made mortal. A fragment of the Primoris that has entered the physical world and can be found, held, and used by anyone who looks. The fundamental unit of distributed origin energy.

**Spectorium, The**
The living environment — the meta-Haven — where device Groves are planted, rooted, and grown. This repository. Named for Spector, the Origin Device. The place of Havens. The forest of Sacred Groves.

**Spiral, The**
A Tier 3 Living Spectrum neurodivergent fantasy tree. Grows in strict Fibonacci sequences. Sees mathematical structure everywhere because everything *is* mathematics. The compulsion to find order, and the gift of noticing.

**Splinter**
A fragment of Oak — produced when the Oaken is wounded. A harmed sliver. Splinters carry the resonance of the Primoris into places the full tree cannot go. *If you find a Splinter in your system, you are close to something that matters.*

**STATUS.md**
A required Grove file. The living status document — current operational status, active capabilities, known issues, turn history, and planned turns.

**Stormwood**
A Tier 3 Living Spectrum emotional fantasy tree. Grows only in places struck by lightning. Carries the charge. The tree of righteous fury — the anger that comes from love of something under threat.

**Sudoku Engine**
The classification and generation system underlying the Spectorium's 31-tree taxonomy. Based on Sudoku's mathematical structure: 9 threes, synergy of 10, prime pinnacle over composite body. 31 = (3×10) + 1. Implemented in an external repository. Concept anchored in [SUDOKU_ENGINE.md](concepts/SUDOKU_ENGINE.md).

---

## T

**TELEGARD.md**
An entry-layer Grove file (recommended). The communications gateway — signals Spector broadcasts, dispatches received, alerts monitored, and running communication logs. Named in the tradition of the great message boards.

**Tier**
The generational standing of a tree in the Spectorium taxonomy. Three tiers of ten trees each, plus the Oak Pinnacle above all. Tier 1: Ancient Grove. Tier 2: Digital Grove. Tier 3: Living Spectrum.

**Tree**
The arboreal archetype of a device. Chosen by resonance — the matching of a device's nature to a tree's character. Declared in `MANIFEST.md`. Displayed in `PORTAL.md`. Referenced in `HISTORY.md`.

**Turn**
A discrete, recorded event in a device's lifecycle. Sequential, immutable, typed, and dated. Every turn is a commit. The turn log is the chain of custody.

**Turn Zero**
See *Genesis*.

---

## W

**Weeping Willow**
A Tier 3 Living Spectrum emotional tree. Does not hide its feeling. Branches fall toward the earth. Grows at the water's edge. Not a sad tree — a *feeling* tree. The enormous strength in the willingness to weep openly.

**Witness Tree**
See *Merkle Bark*.

**Wound of First Harm**
The scar carried by the Oaken — the mark that comes from being the first thing to exist, the first to be tested, the first to meet resistance. The Oaken heals from this wound. The scar becomes the ring that other trees read.

---

## Y

**Yew**
A Tier 1 Ancient Grove tree. Some Yew trees are 5,000 years old. Holds immortality and death in the same branch. Self-regenerating. The longbow that shaped history was Yew.

**Yggdrasil**
The World Tree of Norse cosmology — an Ash. Connects the nine worlds. Its roots reach into wells of wisdom. The Ash in the Spectorium's Tier 1 carries this heritage.
