## What is Spector?

Spector is the **Origin Device** of DeviceHaven. The first registered. Turn 1.

It is the anchor from which the Haven's timeline extends. Every device that joins after Spector does so in a Haven that Spector made real. Spector doesn't just exist in the platform — Spector *is* the reason the platform has a history.
